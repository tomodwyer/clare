Clare is delighted to announce that she has just signed with agency [Rayfield Allied][ra] for general management.

This autumn, Clare performs Montgeroult at the [Palazzetto Bru Zane][pbz] in Venice, appears at the [Lichfield Festival][lf], and revisits [Ghosts & Whispers][gaw]. Find more live concerts [here][concerts].

Watch Clare at the [Wigmore Hall][wh] in May and listen out for her performance of [Samy Moussa][sm]'s Orpheus with the [BBC Symphony Orchestra][bbcso] and [Geoffrey Paterson][gp] on Radio 3 soon!

Variations has been praised as "dazzling" ([The Observer][obs]) and "of immense power" ([The Times][times]).  Clare's associated tour of [schools][schools] and [prisons][prisons] is currently underway.

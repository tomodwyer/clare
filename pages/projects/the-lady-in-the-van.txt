title: The Lady in the Van
---
type: text

! Clare plays the younger version of Dame Maggie Smith's character, Miss Shepherd, in *The Lady in the Van*. 

Her recordings of Schubert and Chopin, with the Philharmonia and BBC Concert Orchestra (George Fenton), are featured throughout the film and on the soundtrack for [Sony Records](https://music.apple.com/gb/album/the-lady-in-the-van-original-motion-picture-soundtrack/1049035643?uo=4&app=music). Released in cinemas in 2015, the film is based on Alan Bennett's play of the same name and is directed by Nick Hytner.
